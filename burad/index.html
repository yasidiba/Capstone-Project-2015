Welcome to Burad Chemicals Pvt. Ltd.

We manufacture and trade chemical products related to ceramic and rubber industry.

We Manufacture

We manufacture a diverse and extensive link of Anti-tac Agents, designed to reduce adhession between rubber surfaces. We supply both liquid and powder form of material. Our products are Bio-Degradable and do not retard rubber properties. We maintain the quality standards during the entire process of manufacturing.

We Trade

We are a supplier of Monofilament Yarn of Nylon and Polypropylene produced on sophisticated German machines with high Quality Standards. We can also cater any specific requirement of Monofilament Yarn for specialized applications like Geo Textiles, Grass Trimmer, Bristles, Agro Lines, etc.

Our Basic Product Line

We basically trade in minerals that are consumed in the manufacturing of Rubber compounds, Rubber products like Tyres, Conveyor Belts, Hose Pipes, Rubber Sheets, etc. and in minerals that are consumed in the manufacturing of Ceramic Tiles, Sanitary Wares, Vitrified Tiles, Platforms, etc
